Featured Products

Some of the items we sell

Premium quality industrial and mechanical components . Each product is engineered for durability, reliability, and performance in demanding environments.

U Bolts

High-strength U-shaped bolts for securing pipes, cables, and equipment to structural supports. Used in MEP systems, HVAC installations, and industrial piping.

U Clamps

Versatile pipe clamps for suspending and supporting piping systems. Available in various sizes for liquid, gas, and steam lines.

MS Pipe Fittings

Mild steel pipe fittings including elbows, tees, reducers, and couplings for industrial and construction applications.

Pipe Hangers

UL & FM approved pipe supports for fire sprinkler systems, HVAC, and industrial piping. Engineered for safety and longevity.

Strut Channel

Open-web structural framing for flexible support systems. Used for equipment mounting, cable tray support, and MEP installations.

Wall Brackets

Heavy-duty wall-mounted brackets for pipe and equipment support. Ideal for plumbing, electrical, and HVAC routing.

Nut, Bolt & Washer

Complete fastening solutions including hex bolts, nuts, lock washers, and flat washers in various grades and sizes.

Swivel Clamp

Adjustable swivel pipe clamps allowing flexible pipe positioning. Perfect for tight spaces and complex routing requirements.

Teflon Tape

PTFE thread seal tape for leak-free pipe connections. Essential for plumbing, gas lines, and high-pressure systems.

J Bolts

J bolts are curved metal fasteners with threading, commonly used to anchor structures into concrete.

P Bolts

P bolts feature a closed loop head, commonly used to securely hang pipes or cables.

Threaded Rods

Threaded rods are long, fully threaded metal fasteners used to connect materials under high tension.

Square U Bolts

Square U bolts have a flat bottom, designed perfectly to clamp around square metal tubing.
